# Vertical Hello World

### Trials

This is your first trial.
Every chapter has at least one trial.
One trial is almost always placed at the very end, and uses everything learned throughout the chapter.
Completing every trial in a chapter completes the chapter, even if you haven't completed every lesson.

## The Task

In this trial, you will use 4 lines of code to print 12 lines of text.
The text you are to print is:

    H
    e
    l
    l
    o

    W
    o
    r
    l
    d
    !

> [!TIP]
> Remember to use capital letters and don't forget the `!`. Your text should be identical to the one above.

You can use `print`, `\n`, or `printn` as you wish.
Good luck.
